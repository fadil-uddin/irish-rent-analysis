# Data Sources

## Primary Source: RTB/ESRI Rent Index / Average Rent Dataset

Source: Residential Tenancies Board / Economic and Social Research Institute

Purpose: Analyse rent trends across Irish locations and property types.

Notes:
- The RTB/ESRI Rent Index tracks Irish rental market price developments over time.
- It is based on RTB tenancy registration data independently analysed by the ESRI.
- The RTB data hub provides rent datasets that can be used to analyse average rents by location, quarter, and property type.

Download date:
- To be filled in when the dataset is downloaded.

Dataset limitations:
- Coverage depends on registered tenancies.
- Some locations may have missing or suppressed values due to insufficient data.
- Rent values describe recorded tenancy data and should not be interpreted as full affordability measures without income data.
- The project does not claim causality.
